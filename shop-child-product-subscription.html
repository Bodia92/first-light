Product Details

You can’t beat a Tenderloin. The Tenderloin is tucked under the ribs where it does as little work as possible. It is the most tender muscle on the animal and never lets you down which is why people love it.

Cut it into filet steaks or roast it whole. Whatever you choose Tenderloin is always a winner.

This is a whole untrimmed wagyu tenderloin. Beautifully tender in every bite. Cut into steaks or roast it whole.

This Tenderloin has been graded marble score 4-5. This indicates the amount of marbling in it. MBS 4-5 flavor profile is classed as medium in regards to richness.

Delivered FROZEN. If you would like to cut this tenderloin into filet steaks, let thaw until possible to cut. Vacuum pack the filets. You can then refreeze them until ready to eat.

From farm to table

We are a small New Zealand farming co-operative quietly changing the world. Back in the early 2000s we made the decision to meet an unfulfilled demand for tender, juicy, healthy meat.

Our promise is to produce and provide you with delicious meat, raised right, by people who truly care. We know we do things the right way but we also value the opinions of those we respect. We have therefore partnered with like-minded organisations, to validate and authenticate what we do.
