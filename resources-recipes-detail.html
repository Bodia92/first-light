Lemon Garlic Cream

  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard

  • tsp finely chopped lemon zest
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 pinches salt
  • Pepper
Brussel Sprouts
  • 300g Brussels sprouts*
  • 1 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 4 pinches salt
  • tsp paprika
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 Tbsp finely grated parmesan
  • 1 Tbsp panko breadcrumbs
